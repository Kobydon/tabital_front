Tabital Installment Formulas

Down Payment (D) D = P × 40%
Remaining Balance (B) B = P - D + F
Installment Amount (I) I = B / (N - 1)
Late Fee (LF) LF = O × 10%
Merchant Payout MP = P - (P × 10%)
Total Payable Total = P + F + LF
